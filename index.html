Voice Command Rules

  • To Move a Pawn: -a pawn to a4, b pawn to b4
  • To Move the King's Rook: -king rook to a1
  • To Move the Queen's Rook: -queen rook to a8
  • To Move the King's Knight: -king knight to b1
  • To Move the Queen's Knight: -queen knight to b8
  • To Move the King's Bishop: -king bishop to c1
  • To Move the Queen's Bishop: -queen bishop to c8
  • To Move the White King: -white king to e1
  • To Move the Black King: -black king to e8
  • To Move the White Queen: -white queen to d1
  • To Move the Black Queen: -black queen to d8
  • To Make an Enpassant Move: -a enpass to 'position'
