Voice Command Rules

  • To Move a Pawn: a-pawn to a4, b-pawn to b4, c-pawn to c4...etc
  • To Move the King's Rook: king-rook to a1, king-rook to h1
  • To Move the Queen's Rook: queen-rook to a8, queen-rook to h8
  • To Move the King's Knight: king-knight to b1, king-knight to g1
  • To Move the Queen's Knight: queen-knight to b8, queen-knight to g8
  • To Move the King's Bishop: king-bishop to c1, king-bishop to f1
  • To Move the Queen's Bishop: queen-bishop to c8, queen-bishop to f8
  • To Move the White King: white-king to e1
  • To Move the Black King: black-king to e8
  • To Move the White Queen: white-queen to d1
  • To Move the Black Queen: black-queen to d8
